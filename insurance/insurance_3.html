Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana

This is an accident Insurance scheme, PMSBY offers a one-year accidental death and disability cover, which can be renewed annually.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Farmers must have a Savings Account (or Jan-Dhan Account) .
  • The Criteria of selection of Implementing Agency and area allocation will be same as PMFBY.
  • Farmers belonging to any Indian state are eligible under this scheme.

Documents required:
  • Bank Passbook
  • Aadhar Card
  • PAN Card

Toll-Free Helpline Number:
  • Number : 019-23654092
  • Email: pmsby-insurance@gov.in

Registration Website: http://www.dif.mp.gov.in/pmjsby.htm?
