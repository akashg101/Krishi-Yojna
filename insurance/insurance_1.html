Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

This scheme aims to provide comprehensive insurance cover against failure of the crop thus helping in stabilising the income of the farmers.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Farmers belonging to any Indian state are eligible under this scheme.
  • Farmers must have a Savings Account (or Jan-Dhan Account) .

Documents required:
  • PAN Card
  • Bank Passbook
  • Aadhar Card

Toll-Free Helpline Number:
  • Number : 011-23381092
  • Email: help.agri-insurance@gov.in

Website: https://pmfby.gov.in/
